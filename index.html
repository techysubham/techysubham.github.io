Accident Prevention using OpenCV

There are a lot of accidents taking place every day due to mishaps. As the population grows the no. of cars and accidents is directly proportional. This example program shows how to find frontal human faces in an image and estimate their pose. The pose takes the form of 68 landmarks. These are points on the face such as the corners of the mouth, along the eyebrows, on the eyes, and so forth.

Python OpenCV Dlib Shape Predictor 68 Face Landmarks

Key Features:

  • 68-Point Facial Landmark Detection
  • Real-Time Drowsiness Detection
  • Audio Alert System
  • Live Video Processing
